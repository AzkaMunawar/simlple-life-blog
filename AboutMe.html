My name is sara and living the simple life saved my life.

I used to be a pack-rat. I had so many things that I think I loved, but truth was I couldn't appreciate any of it beacuse it was too much.

Things were terrible. I had a job that I hated, home I don't want to live in and I couldn't hold a good relationship with friends and family.

How I turned things around.

I was on a verge of breakdown when I got rid of everything. I sold my palce, quit my job and rented out a small apartment.

It was not easy at all. But everything was in such a bad place that I cann't figure out anything else. And it worked for me.

Now I live a simple life

Now that I am living a simple life, things are so much better. I am less stressed, enjoy life and work more, and I have more free time to enjoy.

So, I now feel like I should start sharing with others that how I cahnged my life. I want everyone to benefit from a simple life.
